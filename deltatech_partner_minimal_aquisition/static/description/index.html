Partner Minimal Aquisition

Features:

  • Adds a field for minimal acquisition value on partner form
  • On the purchase order, the system checks if the partner has a minimal acquisition value and if the total amount of the order is less than the minimal acquisition value, the system will show a warning as a banner on top of the order.
